About Calisphere

Welcome to Calisphere: your gateway to California’s remarkable digital collections. Calisphere provides free access to unique and historically important artifacts for research, teaching, and curious exploration. Explore photographs, documents, letters, artwork, diaries, oral histories, films, advertisements, and more.

The collections you’ll find on Calisphere have been digitized and contributed by all ten campuses of the University of California and other great libraries, archives, and museums throughout the state.

Calisphere beta: more content, better access

Calisphere beta is the latest version of the Calisphere website. It’s in “beta” mode because we’re still actively developing it—but we couldn’t wait any longer to share it with you. Calisphere beta has more content than ever before and new features to help you hone in on the materials you need. .

If you’re a longtime Calisphere visitor, you may notice the absence of curated sets of resources such as Themed Collections, JARDA, and California Cultures. We are actively working on adding these to Calisphere beta. In the meantime, you can access them through the old Calisphere website.

We’d welcome your feedback on the Calisphere beta site.

Our Organization

Calisphere and Calisphere beta are projects of the University of California Libraries, developed and maintained by the California Digital Library. For more information about Calisphere beta and its underlying technical architecture, visit our documentation site.

Join us!

Calisphere aggregates digital content and metadata from established collecting institutions in California. If you represent such an organization and are interested in contributing digital content to the site, please contact us.
